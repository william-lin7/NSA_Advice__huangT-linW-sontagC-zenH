Welcome to our website! We are dedicated to providing you random information regarding your surroundings. All you need to do is enter a valid address in the format "[street_number] [street] [city]"! Don't worry, your information is only used for your benefit. So enjoy our website!!

APIs Used
Location IQ
- returns the latitude and longitude of an address

Places API
- takes in location as latitude and longitude and returns places nearby

Google Civic Information
- provides civic information based on residential address

OpenWeatherMap
- provides weather data given geographic location

Maps Embed
- takes in location as latitude and longitude and returns a google map of it
