Here's how to make the most out of our website!


1. Register for an account
2. Log in with your new account! Exciting :)
3. Input your city in the location!
4. Input your address in the format "[street_number] [street] [city]"
5. Get your API keys if you don't have them. How do I get them you ask? Great Question!
My Google Civic/Place/Map API key is missing!

Log into your Google account and go to "Credentials" at your Google Developer Console, where you can start a new project to get a key! Luckily for you, this key works for all google APIs. Just remember to enable them in your account!

My OpenWeather key is missing!

Create an account at OpenWeather. Once you've signed in, go to API keys, where you should find a key already generated for you!

My LocationIQ key is missing!

Sign up at LocationIQ. Once you're logged in you'll be able to find your token on the Geocoding Sandbox!

6. Now explore our random buttons and have fun!
